# Auto Loan Securitization Risk Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing auto loan securitization data to identify credit risk, delinquency trends, loan performance, and portfolio stability. An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to transform raw financial data into actionable business insights for effective risk management and faster decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Financial institutions faced challenges in monitoring auto loan portfolios and identifying risky borrower segments that could impact securitized assets and overall financial performance.

Key business challenges included:
- Increasing loan default risk
- Difficulty tracking delinquent borrowers
- Poor visibility into portfolio performance
- Delayed financial reporting and risk assessment
- Lack of centralized KPI monitoring
- Difficulty identifying high-risk customer segments

These challenges increased financial exposure and reduced operational efficiency for business stakeholders.

---

## Objective

The objective of this project was to:
- Analyze auto loan portfolio performance
- Identify high-risk borrower segments
- Track delinquency and default trends
- Monitor important financial KPIs
- Improve business decision-making using data visualization
- Support proactive risk management strategies

## Problem Solving Approach

To solve business challenges in auto loan securitization risk management, a structured analytics approach was followed.

### Step 1: Data Collection & Cleaning
- Collected and organized raw loan portfolio data
- Removed inconsistent and duplicate records
- Standardized financial datasets for accurate analysis

### Step 2: Risk Identification
- Analyzed delinquency and default trends
- Identified high-risk borrower segments
- Evaluated loan categories with maximum portfolio exposure

### Step 3: KPI & Financial Analysis
Created and analyzed key financial KPIs such as:
- Default Rate
- Delinquency Ratio
- Average Loan Balance
- Interest Rate Trends
- Portfolio Risk Exposure

### Step 4: Dashboard Development
Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to:
- Monitor loan portfolio performance
- Track repayment behavior
- Analyze financial risk indicators
- Support data-driven decision-making

### Step 5: Business Recommendations
Provided actionable recommendations to:
- Reduce default risk
- Improve loan approval strategies
- Optimize portfolio diversification
- Strengthen financial monitoring processes

## Key Insights

- Certain borrower segments showed higher default probability
- Delinquency rates increased among low-credit-score customers
- High loan amounts carried increased repayment risk
- Some regions contributed higher portfolio risk exposure
- Repayment performance varied significantly across customer categories

---

## Business Recommendations

- Strengthen credit risk assessment before loan approval
- Reduce exposure to high-risk borrower segments
- Improve monitoring of delinquent accounts
- Optimize interest rate strategies based on risk profiles
- Enhance portfolio diversification to minimize financial risk

---

## Business Impact

This dashboard helps financial institutions to:
- Improve risk management decisions
- Monitor loan portfolio health effectively
- Identify high-risk accounts early
- Reduce financial losses from defaults
- Improve operational efficiency through centralized reporting
